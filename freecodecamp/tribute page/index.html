Dr. Norman Borlaug - The Father of the Green Revolution

Norman Ernest Borlaug was an American agronomist who led initiatives worldwide that contributed to the extensive increases in agricultural production termed the Green Revolution. He was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 1970 for his contributions to world peace through increasing food supply.

Borlaug is considered to be one of the most significant agricultural scientists in history and is often referred to as the "Father of the Green Revolution."
